WebGL Car Drive

Controls:

  • Use cursor keys to move the car.
  • Use W, A, S, D, shift, space to move the camera.
  • Use page up and page down to zoom in and out.
  • Use T for selecting different Textures for the wall.
  • Use F for selecting a Racing-Track.
